About Tahra

Where clinical knowledge meets compassionate, practical support.

Tahra brings hands-on experience from hospital and community settings, including Wadi Halfa Teaching Hospital, Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF), and SUDRO Organization. Her work combines individual counseling with nutrition assessment, health education, CMAM support, and follow-up care.

Her professional goal is simple: make sound nutrition guidance understandable, culturally responsive, and useful for the people and communities receiving it.
